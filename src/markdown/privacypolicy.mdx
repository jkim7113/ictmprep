# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 11/15/2024

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to [ictmprep.com](https://www.ictmprep.com) ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the safety of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## 2. Information We Collect
To provide our services effectively, we may collect the following types of information:

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, login, password recovery, and important updates.
- **Username**: Used to identify you on the platform.
- **Password**: Used to secure your account (encrypted and stored securely).

### Performance Data
- **Question Performance**: Your results in various categories of practice questions to help track your progress and tailor recommendations.
- **Time Taken**: The duration it takes for you to solve questions on practice exams, used to analyze your speed and provide insights.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Account Management**: To create and manage your user account, including login and password recovery.
- **Performance Tracking**: To monitor your progress and provide personalized recommendations to improve your skills.
- **Communication**: To send you updates, important information, and responses to any inquiries or support requests.
- **Analytics and Improvement**: To analyze user performance data and improve the content and features of the website.

## 4. How We Protect Your Information
We take your privacy and data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your password and secure our servers. Access to your personal information is limited to authorized personnel only.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

- **Legal Compliance**: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations.
- **Service Providers**: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing our services, but only to the extent necessary.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:

- **Access and Update**: View and update your personal information in your account settings.
- **Delete Your Account**: Request the deletion of your account and associated data in your account settings.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking
We may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain features.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
